How QR ordering works

Your team generates a secure QR per table. Guests scan, order, and follow the status timeline.

  1. Generate QR for Table (e.g., A5)
  2. Guest scans → opens menu instantly
  3. Guest orders → server creates order
  4. POS receives event → prints kitchen ticket
  5. Guest tracks: Open → In kitchen → Ready → Served
